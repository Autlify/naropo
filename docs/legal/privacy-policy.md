## 7. Your Rights Under PDPA

You have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right of Access (Section 30)
Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### 7.2 Right of Correction (Section 34)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 7.3 Right to Withdraw Consent (Section 38)
Withdraw consent for processing (note: may affect service availability).

### 7.4 Right to Limit Processing (Section 40)
Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 7.6 Right to Complain
Lodge a complaint with the PDPA Commissioner if you believe we violated PDPA.

**To exercise your rights:**  
Email: pdpa@autlify.com  
Subject: "PDPA Data Subject Request - [Your Name]"  
We will respond within **21 days** (PDPA requirement).

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Technical Measures:**
- TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Bcrypt password hashing (12 rounds)
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scans

**Organizational Measures:**
- Role-based access controls
- Data minimization principles
- Employee confidentiality agreements
- Incident response procedures

**No system is 100% secure.** In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and the PDPA Commissioner within **72 hours** as required by law.
