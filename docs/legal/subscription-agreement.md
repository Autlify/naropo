## 4. Billing and Payment

### 4.1 Billing Cycle
Subscriptions are billed **monthly in advance** on the anniversary of your subscription start date.

**Example**: If you subscribe on January 15, you will be billed on the 15th of each month.

### 4.2 Payment Method
Payments are processed via **Stripe, Inc.** We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Other payment methods supported by Stripe in Malaysia

We do **not** store your payment information. Stripe handles all payment data securely (PCI-DSS Level 1).

### 4.3 Automatic Renewal
Your subscription **automatically renews** each month unless you cancel. By subscribing, you authorize us to charge your payment method on each billing date.

### 4.4 Payment Failures
If payment fails:
- We will attempt to charge your payment method **3 times** over 7 days
- You will receive email notifications of failed payments
- If payment remains unsuccessful, your subscription will be suspended
- You will have 7 days to update your payment method before account termination

### 4.5 Currency
All fees are in **Malaysian Ringgit (MYR)**.

### 4.6 Price Changes
We may increase subscription fees with **30 days' written notice**. Price changes take effect on your next billing date. You may cancel before the increase takes effect.

## 8. Upgrades and Downgrades

### 8.1 Upgrading Your Plan
You may upgrade to a higher plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately:
- You will be charged the prorated difference for the current billing period
- Your next full billing cycle will be at the new plan price

**Example**: You upgrade from Starter (RM 79) to Basic (RM 149) on day 15 of your billing cycle. You will be charged RM 35 immediately (half of RM 70 difference) and RM 149 on your next billing date.

### 8.2 Downgrading Your Plan
You may downgrade to a lower plan at any time. Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing period**:
- No immediate charge or refund
- Next billing cycle will be at the new (lower) plan price
- If downgrade causes you to exceed plan limits (e.g., 4 sub-accounts on Starter plan), you must reduce usage before downgrade takes effect

### 8.3 Plan Limit Enforcement
If you exceed plan limits after downgrade:
- We will send a warning email
- You will have 7 days to comply (delete excess sub-accounts, remove team members, etc.)
- If you do not comply, your plan will automatically upgrade to accommodate your usage
